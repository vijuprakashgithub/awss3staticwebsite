About Me

I hold a Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering, bringing over 14 years of teaching and research experience to my current role as a Senior Lecturer at the School of Computing and Innovative Technologies, British University Vietnam.

My areas of expertise span cloud technologies, cybersecurity, and innovative tech solutions, where I actively engage in both academic and applied research. I am passionate about helping students navigate and master these critical fields.

Why I Chose This Profession

Teaching has always been a calling for me, driven by my desire to share knowledge and inspire others. I find immense satisfaction in guiding students through complex topics, helping them build a strong foundation in computing and cybersecurity. This field is constantly evolving, and I am motivated by the challenge of staying at the forefront of innovation and imparting these insights to my students.

My Teaching Philosophy

I believe that the best learning experiences are built on engagement, curiosity, and hands-on practice. My goal as an educator is to foster an environment where students are encouraged to ask questions, think critically, and apply what they learn in practical ways. By incorporating real-world scenarios into my teaching, I aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

My Vision for the Future

As technology continues to shape our world, my vision is to contribute to a generation of students who are not only proficient in technical skills but are also equipped with ethical and critical thinking abilities. I am committed to nurturing innovators and leaders who can contribute positively to society while excelling in their fields.
